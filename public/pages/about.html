ABOUT

I am a person who is interested in all aspects of information technology. I believe that the greatest things ever will be the product of the efforts of people in this field.

I studied Economics at Georgetown University and retain an interest in bringing market forces (aka intelligence) to previously unintelligent marketplaces, in addition to Economics generally.

I recently finished a web development bootcamp, App Academy, in San Francisco. I blogged about it here. This was one of the hardest things I have done, and I definitely learned a lot.

Before I moved to Stockholm I worked at Jack & Jones in Denmark, where I lead a small team focused on a variety of projects that highlighted the brand values and goals, while improving the overall experience for our users. That has led me to working with a lot of various things such as: Major brand campaigns, E-Commerce, Mobile, Newsletters, Copywriting, IA, UX etc.

In 2009 a had a short, but great, stint New York City where I had an internship at MW Design Interactive(a digital design boutique) as the final part of my education as a Multimedia Designer.
